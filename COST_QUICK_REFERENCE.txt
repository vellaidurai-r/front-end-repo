ANSWERS TO COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: Will backend on EC2 cost MORE than current?
A: NO - Same instance, no extra cost

Q: Do I need 2 EC2 instances (frontend + backend)?
A: NO - 1 instance handles both (unless you want separation)

Q: If I DO want 2 instances, how much extra?
A: Year 1: Still FREE (free tier covers 2)
   Year 2+: +$16.94/month (2nd t2.micro)

Q: What if I exceed free tier?
A: AWS notifies 30 days before expiration
   Easy to migrate back to Fargate if needed

Q: Is backend automatically FREE if frontend is EC2?
A: YES - Same instance, same cost

Q: Can I run database (RDS) for free too?
A: YES - RDS t3.micro is FREE year 1

Q: What happens after free tier ends?
A: Standard pricing kicks in (~$28/month total)

Q: Can I add more instances later?
A: YES - Easy to scale up if needed
